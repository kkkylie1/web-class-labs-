Solo Traveling Diaries

Solo traveling has always been on my to-do list, but I was initially worried about the "what ifs." However, this journey taught me a valuable lesson: just live in the moment! Don’t overthink it—just do it! Traveling solo has broadened my horizons, enriched my perspective, and helped me gain confidence. It’s been a life-changing experience I’ll never forget! To remember my adventures, I’ve used videos and design to create digital diaries. It’s been a fun way to document my experiences!

Tokyo Diaries

This is a collection of collages from my first2 solo trip to Tokyo, documenting my travel achievements. I’ve included photos I took during the trip, with my favorite being the top one. In the top one, I combined my photos with anime elements, inspired by Akihabara, the anime center. I imagined what it would be like if anime characters existed in real life there. For each collage, I selected three main colors to focus on.

Bali Diary

This is a collage from my second solo trip to Bali, designed like a CD album. It’s an Instagram reel that begins with the CD album cover opening and playing. I included my favorite photos from the trip and added emojis, as stickers and handwritten words were trending at the time. I also added a sticker to the CD box for authenticity. The video starts with the CD being removed from the box and placed in the player, then it spins as the player turns on (changing from blue to yellow).

Photography

Photography allows me to express my creativity and storytelling abilities. While I don’t have formal achievements to prove it, I consider my progress in photography a personal success. I’ve learned camera techniques, like low shutter speed, to convey my story. In addition to camera skills, I’ve learned to edit photos using Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom. I enjoy enhancing my concert photos to highlight the lighting and energy of live performances. My editing style uses bold colors to make subjects stand out, reflecting the vibrant atmosphere I experienced on stage.
