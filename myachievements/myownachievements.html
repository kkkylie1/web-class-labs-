About Me

Hi! I’m Kylie Leung. Right now, I’m a college student studying Media Production—a subject I’ve always been passionate about. Initially, I hesitated and chose to major in business instead. But soon I realized that business just wasn’t for me. I started asking myself, “If I had chosen media, would I regret it?” The answer was an emphatic NO! But then I flipped the question around: “If I stick with business, will I regret that choice?” And honestly, the answer was YES. I knew I’d constantly wonder about the “what ifs” and how happy I might be pursuing what I truly love. Now, I can say that studying media is one of the best decisions I’ve made. I absolutely LOVE it! This path feels right, and I’m excited to see where it leads me.

Apart from design I also love to make scrapbook, travel, explore different cultures, attend concerts, and take photos, also photograph my friends in my spare time.
